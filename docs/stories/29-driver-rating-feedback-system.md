# User Story: 29 - Driver Rating and Feedback System

**As a** driver,
**I want** to rate customers and receive feedback on my service,
**so that** I can maintain service quality and build reputation on the platform.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Rate customers (1-5 stars) after trip completion
* Provide optional comments about customer behavior
* View my overall driver rating and recent customer feedback
* Rating trends displayed with performance insights
* Ability to report problematic customers to platform administrators
* Rating impacts trip matching priority and earning potential

## Notes

* Two-way rating system balances driver and customer accountability
* Driver reputation affects visibility in trip matching algorithm
* Performance insights help drivers improve service quality
* Reporting mechanism protects drivers from difficult customers
