# User Story: 12 - Receive Driver Payouts

**As a** driver,
**I want** to receive automated payouts for completed trips,
**so that** I get paid reliably for my services.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Automatic payout calculation after trip completion
* Platform commission deducted transparently
* Payout schedule options (weekly/monthly)
* Clear payout statements showing:
  - Trips completed
  - Gross earnings
  - Commission deducted
  - Net payout amount
* Multiple payout methods supported
* Payout history accessible in driver dashboard

## Notes

* Financial accuracy is critical
* Consider instant payout
