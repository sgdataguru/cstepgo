# User Story: 7 - Register as Passenger

**As a** tourist or local passenger,
**I want** to quickly register using my phone number or email,
**so that** I can book trips without a lengthy signup process.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Simple registration flow with OTP verification
* Can register via phone number or email
* Minimal required information collected
* Optional wallet creation for faster future bookings
* Registration completes in under 2 minutes
* Clear confirmation of successful registration

## Notes

* Keep friction low for tourist users
* Consider social login options in future
