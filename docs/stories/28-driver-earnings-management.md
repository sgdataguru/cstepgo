# User Story: 28 - Driver Earnings Management

**As a** driver,
**I want** to track my earnings and manage payouts from completed trips,
**so that** I can understand my income and receive timely payments for my services.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Real-time earnings display after each completed trip
* Detailed breakdown showing trip fare, platform commission, and net earnings
* Daily, weekly, and monthly earnings summaries with charts
* Payout schedule management (daily, weekly, or on-demand)
* Tax reporting features with downloadable statements
* Earnings history with trip details and customer ratings

## Notes

* Core to driver motivation and platform economics
* Platform commission (15-25%) must be clearly communicated
* Payout integration with local banking systems (Kaspi Pay in Kazakhstan)
* Tax reporting helps drivers with compliance requirements
